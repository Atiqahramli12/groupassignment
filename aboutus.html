3 Million Trees by 2023

We announced our promise to plant three million trees in the next three years through Project RELeaf, a forestry programme in Malaysia that contributes to Nestlé's worldwide ambition to reach Zero Net Greenhouse Gas Emissions by 2050, in September 2020.

Sweet News

Nestlé scientists have created a method that resembles hollowing out sugar crystals rather than solidifying them. There are no artificial sweeteners or unusual plant extracts in this recipe; it's simply a new twist on the basic sweet ingredient: sugar. In an interview with Bloomberg, Nestlé Chief Technology Officer Stefan Catsicas stated that the company is attempting to replicate the complex, natural patterns found in food by spreading sugar more unevenly.

The Pioneer Years

The Anglo-Swiss Condensed Milk Company was founded in 1866, and our journey begins there. In 1867, Henri Nestlé produces a groundbreaking infant food, and in 1905, the firm he created combines with Anglo-Swiss to form the Nestlé Group. During this time, cities expand and commodity costs fall as a result of railways and steamships, resulting in an increase in international consumer goods commerce.
